# Tshegofatso Setup Guide

## Welcome! 🎉

This guide helps Tshegofatso (your business partner) set up their own dashboard to track the shared dropshipping venture. Everything here is non-technical and step-by-step.

---

## What You'll Get Access To

Once set up, you can:
- ✅ View shared venture transactions
- ✅ See your share of monthly profit
- ✅ Approve or dispute transactions
- ✅ See settlement status
- ✅ Sign off on monthly reports
- ✅ Track startup loan repayment progress

---

## Step 1: Get the Dashboard Link

The dashboard is hosted on GitHub Pages. Your partner (Masemula) will share the link with you.

**The link looks like:**
```
https://notmasemula.github.io/masemula-estate-dashboard/
```

Open it in any browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox all work).

---

## Step 2: First-Time Setup

When you open the dashboard:

1. **A unique ID is created for you automatically** — no account needed
2. **Bookmark the page** — your data is linked to this browser
3. **Note your User ID** — shown in Settings (you'll need this for ventures)

> 💡 **Tip:** Use the same browser every time to keep your data. If you use incognito mode, your data won't be saved.

---

## Step 3: Tell Masemula Your User ID

Once you have your User ID:
1. Go to Settings (gear icon in sidebar)
2. Copy your User ID
3. Send it to Masemula via WhatsApp

Masemula will link you to the shared venture.

---

## Step 4: View Shared Ventures

Once linked:
1. Click **Ventures** in the left sidebar
2. You'll see the shared dropshipping venture
3. All transactions will appear here for your review

## How the Money Flow Works

### Monthly Cycle

```
1st of month: Month starts
               ↓
During month: Transactions logged (Shopify/Stripe auto-sync)
               ↓
End of month: Settlement calculated automatically
               ↓
You review: Approve or dispute any transactions
               ↓
Both approve: Settlement confirmed
               ↓
Masemula transfers: Your share via Nedbank
               ↓
You mark as received: Transaction complete
               ↓
Report generated: Signed by both
```

### Your Monthly Settlement

The system calculates:
```
Total Revenue (Shopify + Stripe sales)
- Shared Expenses (50% each)
- Reinvestment (agreed %)
= Net Profit

Your Share = 50% of Net Profit
```

### If There's a Startup Loan

If Masemula covered your initial capital:
```
Your Share = 50% of Net Profit - Loan Instalment
```
This continues until the loan is paid off. You can always see the outstanding balance and payment history in the Ventures tab.

## Approvals & Disputes

### How to Approve a Transaction
1. Go to **Ventures** → **Pending Approvals**
2. Review the transaction details
3. Click **Approve ✓**

### How to Dispute a Transaction
1. Click **Dispute** next to the transaction
2. Add your reason (required)
3. Submit — Masemula will respond

All disputes are logged with full history so nothing gets lost.

---

## Notifications

You'll receive notifications for:
- 🔔 New transactions requiring approval
- 💰 Settlement ready for review
- ✅ Money transferred (pending receipt)
- 📄 Monthly report ready to sign
- ⚠️ Any disputes or issues

**To set up notifications:**
1. Go to Settings → Notifications
2. Add your email address
3. Choose which notifications you want
4. Save

---

## Monthly Report (Sign-Off Required)

At the end of each month:
1. Go to **Reports** in the sidebar
2. Review the monthly summary
3. Click **Sign Report** (your digital signature is recorded)
4. Download PDF for your records

Both Masemula and Tshegofatso must sign before the report is finalized.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**
A: No. It's a web app — open the link in any browser.

**Q: What if I lose my data?**
A: Your data is stored in the cloud (Supabase). As long as you use the same browser on the same device, your data will be there.

**Q: Can I use it on my phone?**
A: Yes! The dashboard works on mobile browsers.

**Q: What if I have a question about a transaction?**
A: Use the **Dispute** function with a note. Don't just reject without explaining — this keeps things transparent.

**Q: When will I get paid?**
A: Payments happen monthly. You'll receive a notification when Masemula transfers your share.

**Q: Can I see previous months?**
A: Yes. Reports are archived and available in the Reports section.
