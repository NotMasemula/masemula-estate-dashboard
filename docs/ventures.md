## Investment Rules

- **Kill:** ROAS below 1.5x — stop immediately
- **Tweak:** ROAS 1.5x – 2x — adjust and test
- **Maintain:** ROAS 2x – 3x — hold and optimise
- **Scale:** ROAS 3x+ — scale aggressively
