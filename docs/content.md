## Content Checklist

### Before Posting

- [ ] Content aligns with brand
- [ ] Good lighting/audio quality
- [ ] Caption written
- [ ] Hashtags researched
- [ ] Call-to-action included
- [ ] Scheduled at optimal time

### After Posting

- [ ] Respond to comments (first hour)
- [ ] Share to stories
- [ ] Monitor engagement
- [ ] Note what worked

## TikTok Strategy (DS)

### Content Pillars

1. **Product showcases** — Clean desk setups
2. **Problem/solution** — Messy desk → organized
3. **Unboxing** — Package arrivals
4. **Behind the scenes** — Entrepreneur life
5. **Trending sounds** — Ride the algorithm

### Hook Templates

- "POV: Your desk finally looks like this"
- "Stop scrolling if your desk is messy"
- "This changed my productivity"
- "Wait for the transformation"
- "Things I wish I knew sooner"

### Best Posting Times

| Time | Day | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| 7am | Weekdays | Morning scroll |
| 12pm | All | Lunch break |
| 6pm | All | After work |
| 9pm | Weekends | Evening wind-down |

## Content Production Workflow

```
Idea → Script/Plan → Record → Edit → Caption → Schedule → Post → Engage
```

- **Saturday morning**: Film week's TikToks
- **Sunday evening**: Edit and schedule
- **Daily**: Post and engage
