Masemula Estate OS

"Build the life, then live it."

Welcome to the Masemula Estate Operating System — a personal command center for managing ventures, time, finances, headspace, and growth.

Overview

The Masemula Estate OS is a single-page dashboard designed to centralize all aspects of life management:

SectionPurpose
DashboardDaily overview, priorities, weather, quick stats
VenturesBusiness tracking (Dropshipping, Music, Brand, Events)
GoalsMilestones and progress tracking
Time & RoutineSchedule, focus timer, daily briefings
FinanceIncome/expenses, savings, investments, accounts
HeadspaceEmotion tracking, journaling, mental wellness
AcademicsStudy tracking, GPA goals, course management
ContentContent calendar and posting checklist
AgentsTask delegation to estate managers
JarvisAI assistant integration

Quick Start

  1. Open the dashboard in any modern browser
  2. Use the sidebar icons to navigate sections
  3. Toggle dark mode with the 🌙 button (top-right)
  4. Data persists in browser localStorage
